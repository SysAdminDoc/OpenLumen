v0.6.0 — Adaptive UI, localization, and a polish pass.

Added:
- Spanish, Portuguese, German, French, and Japanese translations.
- Adaptive navigation: bottom bar on phones, navigation rail on tablets and
  foldables; the Presets screen gains a side-by-side list/detail layout on
  large screens.
- Sleep-countdown notification when an alarm-based schedule is active.
- Automation broadcast so Tasker/Termux can react to filter-state changes,
  plus a "PWM Comfort" preset for OLED users who keep backlight high.

Fixed:
- Light theme is now fully defined (Catppuccin Latte) with readable contrast
  in every screen, instead of falling back to washed-out defaults.
- Backup/restore status messages are now translated, and a corrupt import
  reports a real error instead of "null".
- Location entry shows an inline range hint when a coordinate is out of range.
- Screen readers now announce the selected state of schedule, driver, and
  preset options correctly.
- Solar schedule handles the exact geographic poles correctly.

Security:
- The automation entry point now forwards only its two documented extras
  instead of relaying an untrusted caller's entire intent bundle.

No new permissions. Still fully offline; no telemetry; F-Droid clean.
